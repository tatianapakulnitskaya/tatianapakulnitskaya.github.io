These are just a handful of the people that support NASA missions: Flight Director (FD) leads the flight control team and is responsible for the overall mission; the Flight Director makes all decisions regarding a safe, expedient flight. Communications Officer (Com Officer) serves as primary communicator between flight control and astronauts. Flight Activities Officer (FAO) plans and supports crew activities, checklists, procedures and schedules. Flight Surgeon (Surgeon) monitors crew activities, coordinates the medical operations flight control team, provides crew consultation, and advises flight director of the crew's health status. Weather Officers provides up-to-date information on the potential weather or space weather hazards near the spacecraft. Public Affairs Officer (PAO) provides mission commentary to the news media and the public.
